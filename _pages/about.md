Biography
======
I am a Clinical Data Scientist at the [Biomarker Engineering and Analytics](https://chdr.nl/clinical-studies-development/trial-services/biomarker-engineering-and-analytics) group at [Centre for Human Drug Research](https://chdr.nl/). I work to translate complex clinical data into meaningful medical insights. With a background in signal processing, statistical analysis, and computational techniques, I focus on  discovering and validating new cardiovascular, physiological, and neurological digital biomarkers that may enhance personalized medicine.

In my role, I apply signal processing, computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning to analyze data from multiple sources. This interdisciplinary approach aims to identify biomarkers that contribute to early detection, accurate diagnosis, and better estimates of treatment effectiveness for various conditions.

In addition, I work in creating novel non-invasive biomarkers and their subsequent clinical validation the biomarkers and methods to state-of-the-art gold standards.
